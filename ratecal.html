How it works

I offer flexible and personalised support for tutoring and childcare. New families are welcome to schedule a short introductory call so we can make sure the fit feels good and clarify schedules, goals, and expectations.

Tutoring Rates

  • Intro call (15 min): Free
  • 60 min session: 75€
  • 90 min session: 110€
  • 4-session pack (60 min each): 280€ (save 20€)
  • Portfolio / lab report review: from 50€ (flat rate)

Need sliding scale or small-group pricing? Get in touch.

Childcare Rates

Childcare rates vary depending on location, number of children, and timing (evenings, weekends, or regular weekly care).

  • Childcare: 18–22€ per hour
  • Infant care (0–12 months): 20–25€ per hour
  • Evening or late-night care: +2€ per hour
  • Regular weekly nannying: quoted based on schedule

I’m happy to discuss what works best for your family.

FAQs

Can sessions be online?
Yes — tutoring is available online or in-person.

Do you travel for childcare?
Yes, primarily within Pankow, Prenzlauer Berg, and Mitte.

Cancellation policy?
Free to reschedule with more than 24 hours’ notice. Within 24 hours, a 50% fee may apply.
