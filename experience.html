My Background

I have over ten years of experience supporting children and young people in both creative and academic settings. My approach is calm, intuitive, and relationship-based. I work with infants, toddlers, children, and teens — helping them feel safe, curious, and confident in the world.

Childcare & Babysitting

I am experienced with ages 0–14 and especially enjoy supporting families during transitions: after school, bedtime, moving between homes, or adjusting to new routines. I use a gentle, attentive style and value emotional safety, predictable structure, and creativity.

  • Infant care: feeding, diapering, soothing, sleep support
  • Creative play: drawing, crafts, reading, nature walks, sensory activities
  • After-school support: homework help, calm transitions, snacks, play
  • Evenings and bedtime routines
  • Meal prep and light household help related to childcare

Creative & Practical Experience

I have a strong background in craft and making — skills that naturally translate into engaging, hands-on activities for kids.

  • Drawing, painting, sketchbooks
  • Knitting, sewing, bookbinding
  • Cooking, sourdough, pickling, and simple recipes for kids
  • Nature journaling and observation skills
